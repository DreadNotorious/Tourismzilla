A spectacularly beautiful country that is home to crystal clear blue waters, scenic islands, white sandy beaches and a simple yet profound culture that reflects in the sights and people of this country. Enjoy an amazing tour that takes us on a journey of this charming destination!

Your itenary

Day 1
Destination – Athens (3 Nights)

Guests will meet at Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time and proceed to Athens. ‘Joining and leaving tour’ guests will meet the group directly at Athens Airport / Lunch restaurant/ Athens hotel according to the group schedule. Welcome to Athens – Capital and largest city of Greece. Today we enjoy City orientation tour of Athens wherein we come across Hadrian’s Arch Monument – part of a wall separating the old and new cities of Athens, Royal Palace from outside followed by a visit to Temple of Olympia Zeus – a colossal ruined temple. In the evening we walk through Plaka- the oldest section of Athens. It has a village feel, with narrow cobblestone streets lined with tiny shops selling jewellery, clothes and local ceramics.


Day 2
Athens – Delphi – Athens

Today we visit Acropolis Site and Museum. Then we visit Parthenon– The temple that dominates the hill of the Acropolis. Also known as an enduring symbol of western civilization. Then we proceed to Delphi, here we walk the sacred way and visit Temple of Apollo, Delphi Museum, Delphi Theatre, and Temple of Anthana.


Day 3
Athens

It’s time we explore the famous Greek islands through the Saronic Cruise today. We will cover three of the most beautiful islands of Saronic Gulf – Aegina, Poros and Hydra.


Day 4
Athens – Mykonos (1 Night)

Today we proceed to Mykonos by ferry, on arrival we walk down the Mykonian history "alley", coastal paved road, Manto Square, where the famous monument of Manto Mavrogenous stands then we visit little venice, five in a row windmills, which still stand proudly, Then we pass by Church of "Panagia Paraportiani"


Day 5
Mykonos – Santorini (2 Nights)

Today we proceed to Santorini Island by ferry. Santorini, known as the ‘black pearl of Aegean’ is one of the most spectacular Greek islands.


Day 6
Santorini

Today we take guided tour of the Santorini village in which we see Prophet Elias monastery, Pyrgos village, Megalochori village, The Blue Dome of Santorini, Oia village and the famous sunset point.


Day 7
Departure from Athens

It’s time now to say goodbye to our travel companions. Let us be in contact with each other through email, Facebook, and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!! Tour Concludes.
