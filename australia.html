Sydney, Australia

9 Nights 8 Days

Land of Kangaroos! Welcome to the smallest continent that offers the biggest amount of fun and incredible experiences - Australia. Hop on a journey to the land down under and visit Sydney, Melbourne, Gold Coast, Brisbane and Cairns for stunning landscapes, amazing journeys and unforgettable memories
Tour Highlights
  • The icons of Sydney - Opera House & Harbour Bridge
  • 12 Apostles & Shipwreck Coast at Great Ocean Road
  • The Great Barrier Reef by Glass Bottom Boat Ride
  • The amazing experience of Warner Bros. Movie World

Your itenary

Day 1
Arrival – Sydney (2 Nights)

Guests will meet at Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time and proceed to Sydney. Upon arrival we visit the Sydney Tower Observatory deck which gives a 360 degree view of the city.


Day 2
Sydney

Today we visit the world’s most iconic landmark – The Sydney Opera House from inside followed by an orientation tour of Harbour Bridge, Eastern Suburbs, Bondi Beach, Rocks Area. Now it’s time for us to visit the amazing Sydney Sea Life Aquarium to experience the rich marine life. We stroll around Darling Harbour. Later we enjoy the luxurious Sydney Showboat Dinner Cruise along with the Dance Show while enjoying the amazing view of Sydney Harbour and that of Sydney’s amazing skyline.


Day 3
Sydney – Brisbane – Gold Coast (2 Night)

Today we fly to Brisbane – the third most popular city in Australia. Upon arrival, we enjoy the city orientation tour which includes South Bank Waterfront, Town Hall and Kangaroo Point. Later we proceed to Gold Coast – famous for sun, sand and surf.


Day 4
Gold Coast

Today we visit Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary, world renowned for its wild Rainbow Lorikeet birds and is also famous for Koalas & Kangaroos. Later, we experience the magical world where movies come to life - Warner Bros. Movie World. From heart thumping thrill rides like the Green Lantern Roller Coaster, Batwing Spaceshot, Superman Escape and Wild West Falls...Enjoy all of it! We also enjoy ‘The Lego movie 4D experience’ show.


Day 5
Gold Coast – Brisbane – Cairns (2 Nights)

Today we fly to Cairns. Upon arrival, we enjoy Boomerang throwing followed by aboriginal experience with live cultural performance. Later we visit Sky Rail Rainforest Cableway which takes us on an amazing experience over Australia’s World Heritage listed Tropical Kuranda Rainforest canopy and deep into the forest.


Day 6
Cairns – Great Barrier Reef – Cairns

Today we proceed to the world’s largest coral reef system and Australia’s most remarkable natural gift – The Great Barrier Reef. Later we experience the great vistas of the Barrier Reef through the Glass bottom boat ride and explore the world of corals without getting wet in Semi-Submersible.


Day 7
Cairns – Melbourne (2 Nights)

Today we fly to Melbourne. Today we enjoy the city tram ride. Later we proceed to Philip Island. Witness the very elegant, world famous Penguin Parade in the evening at Phillip Island.


Day 8
Melbourne – Great Ocean Road – Melbourne

Today we drive through one of the world’s most beautiful and naturally maintained destinations – The Great Ocean Road. We get a unique opportunity to see the 12 Apostles, Shipwreck Coast and Port Campbell National Park with a breath-taking Helicopter ride.


Day 9
Melbourne – Departure

Today we enjoy the city orientation tour which includes St. Patrick Cathedral, Town Hall, Parliament House, Captain Cook Cottages and visit Fitzroy garden. It’s time now to say goodbye to our travel companions. Let us be in contact with each other through email, Facebook, and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!! Guests will proceed to the airport at the scheduled group flight departure time and take a return flight to their home town. Tour Concludes
