Your itenary

Day 1
Destination – Port Louis (4 Nights)

Guests will meet at Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time and proceed to Port Louis. ‘Joining & Leaving Tour’ Guests will meet the group as per the schedule of the Day 1 of the itinerary, directly at Port Louis Airport / Lunch restaurant/ Port Louis hotel according to the group schedule. Welcome to Port Louis - the capital of Mauritius.


Day 2
Port Louis

Today we visit Pamplemousses formally known as Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam Botanical Gardens - home to an incredible variety of tropical plants, with many of them being indigenous. Later, we have a panoramic view of the Port Louis city from Citadel Fort. Further, we take the city orientation tour wherein we come across Catholic Church, Mosque, High Court building, Port Louis Theatre, French Colonial buildings, etc. We shall enjoy shopping at Le Caudan waterfront in the evening.


Day 3
Port Louis – Ile Aux Cerfs

Today we enjoy a high speed boat ride to Ile Aux Cerfs Island – the paradise island of Mauritius. Upon arrival, we enjoy the the beauty of the Island on a thrilling Parasailing ride. Later we enjoy swimming in the crystal clear waters of Mauritius. You have a chance to enjoy the aquatic activities like Banana Boat, Under Sea Walk and Jet scooter at an additional expense by yourself. All aquatic activities are subject to weather conditions.


Day 4
Port Louis

Today we visit Chamarel village, wherein we come across Black River Gorge, Seven Coloured Earths and Chamarel Waterfalls, the best waterfalls in Mauritius. Later we visit Curious Corner - A world full of illusions. Further, we take a tour to the south of Port Louis, wherein we visit Trou Aux Cerfs also known as Murr’s Volcano - a dormant volcano but can become active at any time in the next thousands years, Grand Bassin - the Sacred Lake, Shiva Temple and the Model Ship Factory.


Day 5
Port Louis – Mahe (2 Nights)

Today we fly to Mahe. On arrival we enjoy the city orientation tour of the ‘World’s smallest capital’ - Victoria. We view the colonial buildings, Freedom Square, St Paul's Cathedral, Clock tower. Later we visit Mission Lodge – a View Point.


Day 6
Mahe – La digue – Praslin –Mahe

Today we proceed to La digue by boat. La Digue is an island of striking beauty where tall coconut palms and gigantic boulders cradle glorious white sandy beaches. We visit Anse Source d’Argent, one of the most spectacular beaches in the world. Later we proceed to Praslin by boat. We also visit Vallee-de-Mai, the only place on earth where the giant Coco-de-Mer nut grows in its natural state. Further we visit Anse Lazio beach.


Day 7
Departure from Mahe

It’s time now to say goodbye to our travel companions. Let us be in contact with each other through email, Facebook, and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!! Tour Concludes.
